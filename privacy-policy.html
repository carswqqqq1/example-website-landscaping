Privacy Policy

This page explains how a Think Green style website may collect and use contact form submissions, analytics data, and project inquiry details.

Information we collect

When a visitor submits a consultation form, requests a callback, or contacts the business directly, the site may collect name, email, phone number, city, project type, message details, and basic request metadata such as page URL or referral source.

How the information is used

That information is used to respond to project inquiries, prepare estimates, route the request to the right team member, and improve the website experience and lead tracking.

Analytics and cookies

The site may use analytics or similar tools to understand traffic, measure page performance, and improve conversion paths. Browser settings can usually be used to control cookies or similar tracking technology.

Sharing

Information is not sold. It may be shared with service providers that help operate the site, deliver email, host forms, or process analytics and lead routing.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the consultation form or by calling the business directly.
