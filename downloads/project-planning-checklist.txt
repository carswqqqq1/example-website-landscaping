Think Green Landscape Project Planning Checklist

1) Project Vision
- What do you want your outdoor space to do better?
- Prioritize 3 outcomes: entertaining, curb appeal, low maintenance, shade, family use.

2) Service Scope
- Identify likely project type:
  - Landscape Design & Build
  - Hardscaping
  - Outdoor Kitchen
  - Fire Features
  - Artificial Turf
  - Irrigation

3) Budget Direction
- Select a realistic budget range:
  - $10k-$25k refresh
  - $25k-$60k transformation
  - $60k+ luxury build

4) Timeline
- Choose target timing:
  - Planning for later
  - 3-6 months
  - 1-3 months
  - Within 30 days
  - ASAP

5) Site Conditions
- Note any current issues:
  - Drainage/pooling
  - Irrigation performance
  - Dead plant areas
  - Shade/sun challenges
  - Access constraints

6) Must-Have Features
- List required items:
  - Paver patio
  - Fire pit/fireplace
  - Outdoor kitchen
  - Turf
  - Lighting
  - Water feature

7) Inspiration References
- Save 5-10 photos of styles you like.
- Mark material preferences and colors.

8) Consultation Notes
- Write your top questions for the design team.
- Keep HOA or property constraints ready if applicable.
