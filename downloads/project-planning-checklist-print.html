Landscape Project Planning Checklist

Use this checklist before your consultation to clarify scope, budget direction, timeline, and must-have features so the first project conversation is faster and more accurate.

1. Project Vision

  • What do you want your outdoor space to do better?
  • Prioritize 3 outcomes: entertaining, curb appeal, low maintenance, shade, family use.

2. Service Scope

  • Landscape Design & Build
  • Hardscaping
  • Outdoor Kitchen
  • Fire Features
  • Artificial Turf
  • Irrigation
  • Outdoor Lighting

3. Budget Direction

  • $10k-$25k refresh
  • $25k-$60k transformation
  • $60k+ luxury build

4. Timeline

  • Planning for later
  • 3-6 months
  • 1-3 months
  • Within 30 days
  • ASAP

5. Site Conditions

  • Drainage or pooling
  • Irrigation performance
  • Dead plant areas
  • Shade and sun challenges
  • Access constraints

6. Must-Have Features

  • Paver patio
  • Fire pit or fireplace
  • Outdoor kitchen
  • Turf or putting green
  • Lighting
  • Water feature

7. Inspiration References

  • Save 5-10 photos of styles you like.
  • Mark material preferences and color direction.

8. Consultation Notes

  • Write your top questions for the design team.
  • Keep HOA or property constraints ready if applicable.

Think Green serves Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Phoenix, Mesa, Chandler, and nearby Valley communities. Bring this checklist to your consultation to speed up planning and scope alignment.
