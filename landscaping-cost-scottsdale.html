What Does Landscaping Cost in Scottsdale?

Exact pricing depends on scope, utilities, finish level, and site conditions, but these planning tiers help homeowners understand the difference between a refresh, a focused outdoor living project, and a full design-build transformation.

Planning Ranges

Typical Scope Tiers

TierTypical ScopeWhat Usually Changes the Budget
RefreshPlanting updates, irrigation adjustments, gravel or turf replacement, and light hardscape cleanup.Site access, irrigation repair depth, and whether old materials need removal.
Outdoor Living UpgradePavers, fire feature, seating zones, lighting, or a smaller kitchen-ready patio buildout.Material quality, utility routing, demolition, and custom details.
Full Design-BuildCoordinated front and back yard redesign with hardscape, planting, drainage, irrigation, lighting, and features.Overall square footage, grading, retaining needs, and finish complexity.
  • Start with the zone that creates the most daily friction or the biggest resale/value gap.
  • Separate must-haves from second-phase features before requesting quotes.
  • Bring inspiration photos so consultation advice is based on finish level, not vague terms.
  • Ask for material clarity early because substitutions can shift budget and quality fast.
