Pavers vs. Concrete for Arizona Patios

Pavers usually offer better repair flexibility and premium finish control. Concrete can work for simpler scopes, but cracking, patch visibility, and edge quality are usually the deciding factors in Arizona climates.

Get Free Design ConsultationView Hardscaping Service

  • Choose Pavers When

    You want stronger finish detail, easier section-by-section repairs, and a patio that needs to visually anchor the whole yard.

  • Choose Concrete When

    You need a simpler surface with fewer layout transitions and the project scope does not require a higher-end hardscape feel.

  • What Usually Gets Missed

    Base preparation, drainage, edge restraint, and how the patio ties into steps, fire features, and adjacent planting.
