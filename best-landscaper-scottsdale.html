What to Verify First

Minimum Trust Requirements

  • Confirm active Arizona ROC licensing and matching classification for the requested scope.
  • Request insurance and bonding proof before mobilization.
  • Ask for recent project examples in Scottsdale or nearby neighborhoods.
  • Ensure your quote defines scope, materials, exclusions, and change-order process.
  • Require a realistic timeline with milestone communication expectations.

Comparison Matrix

Questions to Ask Every Bidder

Category What to Ask Why It Matters
Design Process Do you provide concept planning before build starts? Reduces rework and keeps scope aligned with your goals.
Material Clarity Which exact materials and brands are included in writing? Prevents substitution issues and budget surprises.
Communication Who is the project contact and update cadence? Improves accountability during active construction.
Timeline Confidence How do you handle schedule shifts and client approvals? Helps avoid stalled projects and unclear next steps.
Post-Completion What warranty and punch-list process do you provide? Protects quality and service after project handoff.
