Outdoor Kitchen Guide

How to Plan an Outdoor Kitchen in Arizona

The kitchen itself is only part of the project. Utility routing, wind direction, shade, prep space, seating distance, and patio flow determine whether the final space actually feels easy to use.

Get Free Design ConsultationView Outdoor Kitchen Service
  • Decide whether the kitchen is mainly for quick grilling or full entertaining with prep and serving space.
  • Plan gas, power, and drainage before hardscape materials are finalized.
  • Think about shade and heat exposure so appliances and seating are usable in summer.
  • Leave enough landing space beside cooking equipment and keep the route to the indoor kitchen efficient.
  • Coordinate the kitchen with fire features, TV placement, and dining layout instead of planning each zone separately.
