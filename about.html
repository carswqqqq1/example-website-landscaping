About Think Green

One Team From Design Through Construction

Think Green is a Scottsdale-based residential landscape design-build company. We started because we kept seeing the same problem: homeowners got handed off between designers, contractors, and suppliers who never talked to each other — and the yard paid for it.

Our Story

Built in Arizona, For Arizona Yards

Think Green started with a simple observation: most landscaping in the Phoenix Valley was designed for somewhere else. Plants that couldn't handle 115-degree summers. Drainage that failed the first monsoon. Hardscape that cracked because no one accounted for how the ground moves here.

We've spent over 15 years learning how Arizona yards actually behave — the caliche layers in North Scottsdale that need proper excavation, the SRP flood irrigation schedules in Arcadia, the HOA palette restrictions across Desert Mountain and Troon North, the elevation changes in Cave Creek that demand real drainage engineering before a single paver goes down.

That knowledge isn't something you pick up from a design book. It comes from being on-site in every season, across every soil type, in every neighborhood we serve.

How We Work

What Sets Us Apart

  • Design-Build Accountability

    Planning, material selections, scheduling, and construction stay coordinated through one team. You're never stuck playing phone tag between a designer who blames the contractor and a contractor who blames the designer. When something needs to change, we handle it internally and update you — not the other way around.

  • Arizona-Specific Material Planning

    We specify materials based on how they perform in Phoenix heat, monsoon rain, and Arizona soil — not how they photograph in a catalog. Travertine, concrete pavers, flagstone, desert-adapted plants, drip systems, and lighting fixtures are all selected with longevity in mind. What looks good at install still needs to look good in five years.

  • Scope Clarity Before Anything Starts

    We don't start a build without a signed agreement that defines the scope, materials, exclusions, and change-order process. No verbal promises, no open-ended estimates. You know exactly what's included before the first stake goes in the ground.

  • Homeowner Communication Throughout

    During every active build, you get clear next-step visibility. We tell you what's happening this week, what's happening next week, and what we need from you before we can proceed. No silence, no surprises, no guessing when your yard will be usable again.
