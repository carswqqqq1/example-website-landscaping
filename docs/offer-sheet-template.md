# Website Offer Sheet — Local Service Business

## Offer Name
Premium Local Service Website — 3-Day Setup & Launch

## Who This Is For
Local service businesses (landscaping, hardscaping, home services, contractors) that need a professional, conversion-focused website to generate more calls and quote requests.

## What They Get

### Phase 1 — Setup & Build (Days 1–3)
- Mobile-optimized homepage with full-bleed hero and dual CTA
- Clear call + quote conversion flow (sticky mobile CTA, consultation drawer)
- Working contact form with email notification to owner + confirmation to client
- Before/after gallery block
- Google reviews block (client-provided reviews)
- Services hub + individual service pages
- Local SEO setup (meta tags, schema markup, sitemap, robots.txt)
- Analytics event tracking (`call_click`, `form_submit`, `consultation_open`)
- Netlify hosting setup + custom domain configuration
- Google Sheets lead log (every form submission auto-logged)
- Password-protected staging link for client review before launch

### Phase 2 — Launch & Handoff (Days 3–5)
- Final revisions based on client feedback
- Production deployment to live domain
- Post-launch QA (form test, mobile check, speed check)
- 30-minute walkthrough call (how to send updates, how to read the lead sheet)
- Handoff doc with login credentials and next steps

### Ongoing — Monthly Upkeep ($250/month)
- Hosting on Netlify (CDN, SSL, 99.9% uptime)
- Form processing + email delivery (Resend or SMTP)
- Google Sheets lead log maintenance
- Minor content updates (text, photos, hours, service list changes)
- Monthly speed + uptime check
- Priority response for any site issues (within 1 business day)
- Annual SEO refresh (meta descriptions, schema review, sitemap update)
