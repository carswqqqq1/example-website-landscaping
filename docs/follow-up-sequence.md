# 3-Touch Follow-Up Sequence — Local Business Outreach

Use this sequence after sending a demo link to a prospect. Keep messages short, direct, and human. Do not pitch features — pitch outcomes.

---

## Touch 1 — Day 0 (Demo Delivery)

**Subject:** Quick website concept for [Business Name]

**Message:**

Hey [First Name] —

I rebuilt your homepage to show how it could generate more calls and quote requests from people searching in [City].

Demo: [demo-link]

Setup is $375 to start, $375 at launch, and $250/month after that for hosting and support. Most clients are live within 3–5 days.

If you want to move forward, just reply and I'll send the intake form + invoice today.

---

## Touch 2 — Day 2 (Audit Screenshot)

**Subject:** 2 quick fixes I noticed on your current site

**Message:**

Hey [First Name] —

I grabbed a quick screenshot audit — there are 2 conversion issues on your current site that are probably costing you calls.

I fixed both in the demo: [demo-link]

Want a 10-minute walkthrough? No pressure — just easier to show than explain.

---

## Touch 3 — Day 5 (Direct Close)

**Subject:** Want this live this week?

**Message:**

Hey [First Name] —

Last follow-up on this.

If you want the site live this week: $375 today to kick it off, $375 when it goes live, then $250/month. I handle everything — you just send me your logo, photos, and a few details.

If the timing isn't right, no problem. I'll close this out.

Either way — reply and let me know.

---

## Agency Notes (Internal)

**Pricing to reference in every touch:**

| Payment | Amount | When |
|---|---|---|
| Initial | $375 | At kickoff (before work begins) |
| Completion | $375 | At launch (before DNS is pointed) |
| Monthly upkeep | $250/month | 1st of each month after launch |

**If a prospect asks about the monthly fee:** Position it as the cost of having a professional handle hosting, updates, and support so they never have to think about their website again. One missed call from a bad site costs more than $250.

**If a prospect wants to pay upfront for the year:** $375 + $375 + ($250 × 12) = $3,750. A small discount (e.g., $3,500 paid in full) can help close if needed.
